Privacy Policy for StopList

Last Updated: October 17, 2025

1. Introduction

StopList ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

  • Tasks/Stop-items: All your tasks are stored locally on your device. We never see or access this data.
  • Reminders: All reminders are stored locally on your device. We have no access to them.
  • No Account Required: StopList does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the app. AdMob may collect and use certain information for advertising purposes:

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Advertising ID (for ad personalization)
  • App usage data (for ad optimization)

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings. For more information about AdMob's data practices, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage

  • All app data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not have servers that store your data
  • We cannot access, view, or recover your data
  • If you delete the app, all data is permanently deleted from your device

5. Permissions

StopList requests the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send you reminders for your stop-items (only if you set them)
  • Internet: Required for displaying advertisements via AdMob

We only use these permissions for their stated purposes and do not collect personal data through them.

6. Children's Privacy

StopList does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not target children.

7. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent any personal information because we do not collect any personal information. The only third party that may collect data is Google AdMob, as described above.

8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • You can delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
  • You can disable notifications in your device settings

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. International Users

StopList is available worldwide. By using the app, you consent to the data practices described in this policy, including AdMob's data collection as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: stoplist.support@gmail.com
